Tournament History

Vidarbha Cricket Association (VCA), has made significant progress in recent years and has achieved notable success in domestic cricket. They won their first-ever Ranji Trophy title in the 2017-18 season. In the 2018-19 season, Vidarbha won the Irani Cup by defeating Rest of India, showcasing their dominance in Indian domestic cricket. The Vijay Hazare Trophy is a domestic one-day cricket tournament in India. Vidarbha has made progress in this tournament as well, consistently qualifying for the knockout stages in recent years.
